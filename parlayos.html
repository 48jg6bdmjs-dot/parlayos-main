About ParlayOS

ParlayOS is a picks and analytics tool built by someone who bets his own parlays and got tired of staking them all the same way. This page exists to make one thing unmistakably clear before you subscribe: a model percentage on a single game is not the same number as your chance of winning a parlay built from several of them.

A "73% model" pick is not a 73% parlay – combining legs multiplies their probabilities. Even stacking eight strong 65% picks leaves ~3% chance the whole ticket cashes. At a typical 57% per leg, eight legs is closer to 1‑in‑90.

Because of that, the founder sizes longer parlays much smaller than short ones – roughly a dime (one‑tenth) of a full stake on 7+ leg tickets – not because the model is wrong, but because the hit rate of a long parlay is genuinely low.
